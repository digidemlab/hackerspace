A hackerspace for democracy and learning

Digidem Lab hackerspace is an open weekly event for everyone who wants to learn and work with IT for a better world.

Be motivated

by meeting others with similar interests. Having people around you who are interested in what you do helps you progress, and you can either work silently or ask others for feedback.

Ask for help

when you're stuck. There are experienced IT-people at the hackerspace who will gladly help you out!
